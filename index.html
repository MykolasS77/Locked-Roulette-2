Hello citizen. Have you ever heard about Locked Roulette? Legend says it's a 1-person "band" from Lithuania. The guy does everything by himself, from writing the song, to recording, to mixing-mastering, to building this promotional website. His musical style can be described as heavy metal with mix of other genres like funk, electronic, indie, pop. He cares little about appealing to the audience and likes to make his songs weird and crazy. So next time you feel weird and crazy, perhaps give Locked Roulette a listen?

How it all started.

Long ago in a galaxy far away, some small dude started attending his small town's music school. He picked up the clarinet, but somewhere along the way found himself listening to rock music. Bands like Green Day, Rise Against, Sum-41, The Beatles and many more inspired this dude to learn guitar by himself. Along the way came interest in writing his own songs, which in turn forced him to learn about audio recording, mixing, mastering.

As the dude grew older, his taste in music changed constantly. "10 000 Days" by Tool was the album that changed it all. Not only he realized how awesome polyrhythms are, but also the gritiness of the band's image. He realized he likes scary stuff in art and that made an influence in his own works. Yet sometimes the dude still likes to make softer, humurous type of music.
